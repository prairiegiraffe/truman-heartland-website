2025 Honorees

Heartland Award Honorees are nominated by Truman Heartland stakeholders and selected by the THCF Board. 

Citizens of the Year are nominated from across the Eastern Jackson County region. The Mayors of each town nominate their Citizen of the Year to be recognized on behalf of their community. These nominees are celebrated with a private Citizen's Reception prior to the Gala, as well as on stage during the Gala.

30th Annual Toast to our towns

Thank You for a Night of Celebration and Community Impact

For 30 years, the Truman Heartland Community Foundation’s Toast to Our Towns Gala has been the premier event celebrating the people and organizations shaping a brighter future for Eastern Jackson, Cass, and Lafayette County.

This unforgettable evening brings together 700+ community leaders, philanthropists, businesses, and friends to honor extraordinary service, exceptional generosity, and transformational leadership in our communities.

About our Annual Toast to our Towns Gala

For 30 years, Truman Heartland Community Foundation has gathered annually to honor and recognize exemplary service from individuals, corporate citizens, organizations, and financial advisors throughout our community. These groups are nominated by our Board and stakeholders throughout our Truman Heartland Family of fundholders, donors and committee members. It is our honor to recognize and celebrate their contributions throughout the event, with 700 of our closest friends and donors.

Master of Ceremonies

Nikki Vivas, the Ponch & Nikki Show

Nikki Vivas is a multifaceted media personality known for her vivacious and engaging presence on both television and radio. She is one half of “The Ponch and Nikki Show” and also a syndicated radio talent in other markets.

A Blue Springs native, Nikki began her media career in 2006 pushing boundaries and empowering women along the way. Nikki has supported various local organizations and emceed events all over! She is a passionate advocate for the American Heart Association, Wayside Waifs and believes in the power of speaking life into women!
