Help Lessen the Impact of the SNAP Funding Crisis

More than 650,000 Missourians rely on SNAP each month, including tens of thousands of families right here in our region. At the same time, the Kansas City metro — home to nearly 30,000 federal employees — saw thousands of local workers go without pay for over a month. That means real families, real neighbors, and real needs. Although benefits have been reinstated, the impact still lingers.
